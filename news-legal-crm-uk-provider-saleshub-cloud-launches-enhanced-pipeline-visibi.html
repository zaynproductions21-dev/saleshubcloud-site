Legal CRM UK Provider SalesHub Cloud Launches Enhanced Pipeline Visibility Features

New automation tools help CRM for UK law firms convert more enquiries this autumn

31 May 2026 · SalesHub Cloud

31 May 2026, UK —

SalesHub Cloud, a legal CRM UK specialist, has announced enhanced pipeline visibility and automated follow-up capabilities designed to help law firms convert more client enquiries during the traditionally busy autumn period. The CRM for UK law firms combines GPT-4o artificial intelligence with SRA-aligned compliance features to streamline client acquisition processes.

The platform's pipeline management system links contacts, enquiries, leads, opportunities and cases in unified kanban and list views, enabling solicitors to track won/loss revenue across all client touchpoints. Automation features include drip sequences across email, SMS, WhatsApp and consent-based AI voice calls, with auto-enrolment triggered by stage changes requiring no coding expertise.

"UK law firms face increasing pressure to convert enquiries efficiently whilst maintaining SRA compliance," said a SalesHub Cloud spokesperson. "Our pipeline visibility tools give managing partners clear oversight of business development activities, whilst AI-powered features like eligibility scoring and risk assessment help fee earners focus on the most promising opportunities."

The software consolidates communications from email, WhatsApp Business API, SMS and voice notes into a single feed, with every conversation logged against individual cases. Additional features include 43 visa route templates, branded intake forms with PDF e-signing capabilities, and colour-coded SLA deadline tracking with escalation alerts. All data remains UK-hosted and GDPR compliant, with 100% isolation per organisation and ISO 27001 readiness.

SalesHub Cloud offers a 14-day free trial with no card required, positioning itself as an accessible solution for law firms seeking to improve their legal CRM UK operations during peak enquiry periods.
