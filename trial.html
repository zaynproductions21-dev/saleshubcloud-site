What you get in your SalesHub Cloud free trial

Your 14-day trial unlocks the full SalesHub Cloud platform — not a stripped-back demo. From the moment you sign up you get a private workspace for your firm with your own pipeline, contacts, cases, and team logins. There's nothing to install: SalesHub Cloud runs in any modern web browser, so your team can start working leads the same day.

During the trial you can import or create contacts, build out your sales pipeline, score and qualify leads, set up automated follow-ups, and connect the channels your clients already use. No credit card is required to start, and you decide whether to subscribe at the end of the period.

Manage your pipeline your way

SalesHub Cloud gives every lead and case a place in a visual, multi-stage pipeline. Drag deals between stages, see what's stuck, and keep the whole team looking at the same source of truth. Each record holds the full history — emails, calls, notes, files, and signed documents — so nothing gets lost when a colleague picks up an enquiry. You can tailor pipeline stages to match how your firm actually works, whether that's an immigration matter, a housing case, or a standard B2B sales cycle.

AI lead scoring and risk flags

Rather than guessing which enquiries deserve attention first, SalesHub Cloud scores leads so your team can prioritise the ones most likely to convert. The AI surfaces case notes, flags risks, and helps you spot stalling deals before they go cold. The goal is simple: less time on admin and triage, more time on the conversations that win work.

Automation and multi-channel follow-up

Following up consistently is where most pipelines leak. SalesHub Cloud automates the repetitive parts — drip sequences, reminders, and stage-based actions — across email and SMS. You set the sequence once and the platform keeps every lead warm without anyone having to remember the next step.

WhatsApp, Facebook and TikTok lead integrations

Your clients message you where they already are. SalesHub Cloud connects to the WhatsApp Business API via Twilio so you can send, receive, and log conversations against a contact or case. Leads from Facebook and Instagram sync automatically through the Meta Graph API, and TikTok leads arrive via real-time webhook with a scheduled fallback. Every lead is deduplicated and assigned automatically, so nothing slips between channels.

E-signing and document templates

Send client agreements and onboarding paperwork without leaving the platform. SalesHub Cloud includes HTML document templates with merge fields, PDF generation, and OTP-verified client signatures — all fully audit-logged. That keeps your paperwork compliant and your client file complete.

Security and UK data residency

SalesHub Cloud is built for UK firms and stores data in the UK. Every record — contacts, cases, emails, files — is tied to an organisation ID, so no data is ever shared or visible across organisations; each team operates in a fully isolated environment. The platform is GDPR-compliant, and you can disable a team member's account with one click, immediately blocking them from logging in and ending all of their active sessions.

Onboarding and migrating from another CRM

If you're moving from a spreadsheet or another CRM, you don't have to start from scratch. You can import your existing contacts and bring your pipeline into SalesHub Cloud during the trial, and our team can help you map your data and set up your stages. Most firms are working live leads within the first day or two.
