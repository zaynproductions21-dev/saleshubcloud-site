SalesHub Cloud Launches GPT-4o AI Features for UK Law Firms' Client Intake Process

New legal CRM UK platform introduces AI-powered case notes and consent-based voice follow-up

31 May 2026 · SalesHub Cloud

31 May 2026, UK —

SalesHub Cloud has launched advanced AI in legal technology features designed specifically for UK law firm client intake, introducing GPT-4o powered case notes, eligibility scoring and consent-based AI voice follow-up capabilities. The CRM for UK law firms platform combines artificial intelligence with SRA-aligned compliance to streamline client acquisition processes whilst maintaining regulatory standards.

The legal CRM UK solution utilises GPT-4o technology to automatically format case notes, provide eligibility scoring and conduct risk assessments for incoming client enquiries. The platform's consent-based AI voice follow-up feature, powered by ElevenLabs technology, enables law firms to maintain contact with existing consenting clients whilst ensuring full compliance with SRA conduct rules and PECR consent requirements.

"UK law firms are increasingly recognising the potential of AI in legal client intake, but require solutions that understand the unique regulatory landscape they operate within," said a SalesHub Cloud spokesperson. "Our platform delivers GPT-4o capabilities whilst maintaining GDPR compliance, UK-hosted data and ISO 27001 readiness, ensuring firms can leverage artificial intelligence without compromising their regulatory obligations."

The platform integrates all client communication channels including email, WhatsApp Business API, SMS and voice notes into a single feed, with every conversation automatically logged against relevant cases. Additional features include 43 pre-built visa route templates, automated drip sequences across multiple channels, SLA deadline tracking with colour-coded escalation alerts, and branded intake forms with PDF e-signing capabilities featuring OTP verification.

SalesHub Cloud offers UK law firms a 14-day free trial with no card required, and ensures 100% data isolation per organisation. The platform addresses the growing demand for AI in legal technology whilst maintaining the compliance standards essential for UK legal practice, positioning itself as an SRA-aligned solution for modern law firm client relationship management.
