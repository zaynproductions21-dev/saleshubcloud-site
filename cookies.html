Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

In short: We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how the platform is used. We don't use cookies for advertising. You can control non-essential cookies at any time.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They are widely used to make sites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners.

We also use similar technologies such as local storage and session storage which work in the same way as cookies. This policy covers all of these.

How we use cookies

Sales Hub Cloud uses cookies for three purposes:

1. Essential cookies

These are strictly necessary for the platform to function. Without them you cannot log in, navigate the application, or use core features. These cannot be disabled.

2. Functional cookies

These remember your preferences (such as your last viewed page, table column settings, or display preferences) to improve your experience. You can disable these but some features may not work as expected.

3. Analytics cookies

These help us understand how the platform is used — which features are popular, where users encounter problems, and how we can improve. Data is aggregated and anonymised. You can opt out.

Third-party cookies

Some pages on our marketing website (saleshubcloud.co.uk) may load third-party content such as embedded booking calendars. These third parties may set their own cookies, which are governed by their own privacy policies. We do not control these cookies.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels on our marketing site.

Managing cookies

Browser settings

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging into Sales Hub Cloud. Guidance for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opting out of analytics

You can opt out of anonymised analytics tracking at any time via your account settings under Privacy & Data, or by contacting us at privacy@saleshubcloud.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or within the platform. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
